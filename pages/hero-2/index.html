Wang Jing "Jinggg" Jie

Crazy Raze Player

Wang Jing "Jinggg" Jie (born July 29, 2003) is a Singaporean player who is currently playing for Paper Rex.

- Was named the 19th best Asian player (2021)

- Was named the best Asian player (2022, 2023)

- VCT 2024: Pacific League Stage 1 Finals MVP and League Champion

- VCT Masters Copenhagen & Valorant Champions 2023 Finalist
