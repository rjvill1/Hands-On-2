Overview

Khalish "d4v41" Rusyaidee (born October 14, 1998) is a
Malaysian player who is currently playing for Paper Rex.
He is a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player.

- Was named the 12th best Asian player (2021)

- Was named 13th best Asian player (2022)

- Valorant Champions Tour 2023 Pacific Initiator of the Year

- Valorant Champions Tour 2023 Pacific Champion

- VCT Copenhagen & Valorant Champions 2023 Finalist
